Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Kunpi Tech ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Kunpi Tech mobile applications and website at kunpitech.in. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our apps or visit our website.

By using our apps or website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Information on how our apps are accessed and used, including device type, operating system version, app features used, and crash reports.
  • Device Information: Device identifiers, hardware model, and mobile network information.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us via the website form, we collect your name and email address.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated, anonymised data through third-party analytics tools to improve our apps.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our apps and website
  • Monitor usage and diagnose technical issues
  • Respond to your support requests and feedback
  • Send important notices about app updates (only if opted in)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Play Services – for app distribution and in-app services
  • Google AdMob – for displaying advertisements in some apps
  • Firebase Analytics – for anonymised usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics – for crash reporting and stability monitoring

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

4. Advertising

Some of our apps display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier and other data to show personalised ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device settings.

5. Data Storage & Security

We do not store personal data on our own servers beyond what is submitted through our contact form. Contact form submissions are stored securely and deleted once your inquiry is resolved. We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect your data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at support@kunpitech.in so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@kunpitech.in.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@kunpitech.in
  • Website: kunpitech.in
